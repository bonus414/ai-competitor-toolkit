## ðŸ’¡ Analysis Questions

After gathering information, answer these:

1. **What is their main competitive advantage?**
2. **Who is their ideal customer?**
3. **What are their weak points or gaps?**
4. **How do they differ from your product?**
5. **What can you learn from their approach?**
6. **What should you avoid based on their approach?**
